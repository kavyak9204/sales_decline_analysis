# 📊 Sales Decline Analysis and Business Optimization

## Project Overview
This project analyzes declining sales and identifies key factors affecting business performance using end-to-end data analytics.

##  Tools Used
- Excel (Power Query)
- Python (Pandas)
- SQL (SSMS)
- Power BI

## Data Cleaning
- Handled missing values and duplicates
- Fixed inconsistent date formats
- Standardized data for analysis

## Analysis Performed
- Created metrics like final price and total sales
- Analyzed sales by region, category, and customer
- Identified top-performing and low-performing products
- Evaluated impact of discounts on profit
- Studied monthly and yearly sales trends

## Key Insights
- Some regions showed low sales performance
- High discounts reduced profitability
- Few customers contributed major revenue
- Certain products underperformed

##  Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing on low-performing regions
- Optimize discount strategy to improve profit
- Improve inventory planning based on demand
- Use customer insights for retention

##  Dashboard
Power BI dashboard created to visualize KPIs such as sales, profit, trends, and regional performance.
